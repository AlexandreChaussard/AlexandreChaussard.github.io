## About me

Since November 2023, I am a PhD candidate in Machine Learning at Sorbonne Université, under the supervision of Sylvain Le Corff, Anna Bonnet and Harry Sokol.

My main research topics include:
- Deep Learning
- Generative models
- Variational inference
- Structured learning
- Microbiome data analysis
